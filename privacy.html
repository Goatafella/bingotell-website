Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

Bingotell is developed by Goatafella Studios. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information we collect

Bingotell does not require an account and does not intentionally collect your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

Camera

The camera is used to scan physical bingo cards. Images are processed for gameplay and are not used for advertising or sold.

Microphone

The microphone is used only when Smart Caller is enabled to recognize announced bingo calls. Audio is used for gameplay and is not sold or used for advertising.

Local app data

Scanned cards, game progress, and preferences may be stored locally on your device so the app can function.

Purchases and service providers

In-app purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. RevenueCat may process purchase and entitlement information needed to unlock or restore Bingotell Pro. These providers handle information under their own privacy policies.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information is shared only when necessary to provide app functionality, process purchases, comply with law, or protect users and the service.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards appropriate to the information handled by the app. Purchase-related communications use secure network connections.

Children

Bingotell is not directed to children under 13.

Your choices

You can revoke camera or microphone permission in your device settings. Some features will not work without the permissions they require.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when changes are published.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to kiddgoatproductions@gmail.com.
