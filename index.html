FAQ

Quick answers.

Does Bingotell require an account?

No. Bingotell does not require you to create an account.

Why does Bingotell need camera access?

The camera is used to scan printed bingo cards and recognize their numbers.

Why does Bingotell need microphone access?

The microphone is used only when Smart Caller is enabled to recognize announced bingo calls.

How do I restore Bingotell Pro?

Use Restore Purchases inside the app. Google Play and RevenueCat are used to validate purchases and restore access.
