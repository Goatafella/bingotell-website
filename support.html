Common troubleshooting

The scanner is not recognizing a card.

Use bright, even lighting, keep the full card inside the camera frame, avoid glare, and hold the phone steady.

Smart Caller is not hearing calls.

Confirm microphone permission is enabled and reduce nearby background noise.

My Pro purchase is not showing.

Open the app settings and use Restore Purchases while signed into the same Google Play account used for the purchase.

I found a bug.

Email us with the steps that caused the issue and any screenshots that may help.
